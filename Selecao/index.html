Who are Mwendar?

Mwendar Real began in 2007 as a small consultancy office in the heart of Nairobi, driven by a vision to provide professional and reliable real estate solutions. Over the years, our dedication, expertise, and commitment to excellence have fueled our growth, transforming us into a large, internationally recognized real estate company. Today, we proudly serve clients both locally and globally, offering top-tier property sales, management, rentals, and development services. Our journey from a modest startup to a trusted industry leader reflects our passion for real estate and our unwavering mission to help individuals and businesses find the perfect properties to meet their needs..

Customers can easily link their PayPal and bank accounts to their Mwendar Real accounts for seamless transactions. To do this, they need to log into their Mwendar account, navigate to the payment settings, and select the option to link a PayPal or bank account.
